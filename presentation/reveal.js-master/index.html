Introduction to MicroFrontEnds

presented by Yuliya Shulgan, the student of the RSSchool

Why?

MicroFrontEnds are becoming a trend for building web-based applications!

The European furniture company employs MicroFrontEnds for their online store experience.

“Two pizza rule.” If a team is too large to feed with two pizzas, the team is simply too large.

Spotify, an online streaming media service, uses MicroFrontEnds for its desktop programs and iframes to put everything together.

What?

MicroFrontEnds are new designs where the web application is fragmented into several applications

For example, possibly implementation of MicroFrontEnds: navigation bar, personal information, saved stories, posts

Like fruits, cut on pieces, are combined into different tasty deserts
